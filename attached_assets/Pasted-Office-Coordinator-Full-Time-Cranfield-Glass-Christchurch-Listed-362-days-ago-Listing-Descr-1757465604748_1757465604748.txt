Description

What You'll Do
Customer Service: Be the friendly face and voice of Cranfield Glass, managing calls and customer enquiries.
Admin Support: Keep our records up to date and ensure data accuracy – your attention to detail is key!
Scheduling: Help coordinate and track job schedules to keep everything running smoothly.
Team Collaboration: Work closely with our field team to ensure smooth coordination between office and on-site activities.
Data Management: Maintain logs and update job statuses – we need someone organised and meticulous.
Walk-In Support: Assist walk-in customers, prepare quotes, and guide them through our services.
Service Coordination: Support the Office Manager with key tasks and initiatives.

Experience: Customer service or admin roles experience is ideal, but if you have the right skills, we’re interested!
Skilled: Excellent with organisation, multitasking, and communication.
Proactive: A go-getter with a can-do attitude, always ready to learn and adapt.
Tech-Savvy: Proficient in Microsoft Office 365, including SharePoint – it's a plus if you can bring those skills, but we are currently developing this, so it's not a deal-breaker.
