Office Administrator / Coordinator – Busy Glazing Business

Cranfield Glass | Christchurch | 20+ Years in Business

Full-time, 5 days/week, 8 hours/day (opportunity to become permanent)

We’re a busy, locally-owned glazing business in Sydenham, Christchurch, and we’re looking for an organised, proactive Office Administrator / Coordinator to join our team immediately. If you enjoy variety, problem-solving, and keeping things running smoothly, this role is perfect for you.

About the Role

You’ll be at the heart of our operations, coordinating multiple small jobs from start to finish. Every day is different, from emergency call-outs to planned installations, so no two days are the same.

Key Responsibilities:

Customer Service: Be the friendly face and voice of Cranfield Glass, managing calls and walk-in enquiries.

Scheduling: Coordinate and track job schedules, ensuring everything runs on time.

Admin & Data Management: Keep records accurate, update job statuses, and maintain logs.

Team Collaboration: Support field staff and office management for smooth operations.

Service Coordination: Help the Office Manager with initiatives and ad-hoc tasks.

Quote Preparation: Assist customers with quotes and guide them through our services.
